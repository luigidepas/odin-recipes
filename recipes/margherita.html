Margherita

Description

By following this simple recipe, you will be able to make a Margherita pizza. This pizza has tomato sauce, mozzarella and a touch of basil.

Ingredients

  • 300g of flour 00
  • 300ml of water
  • Some fresh brewer's yeast
  • A pinch of fine salt
  • 300g of tomato sauce
  • Fresh basil
  • 200g of mozzarella cheese
  • Some virgin olive oil

Steps

  1. Prepare a large bowl and mix the flour with the salt.
  2. Prepare a small bowl of yeast with warm water, and wait for 5 minutes.
  3. Mix the flour with the yeast. While doing so, slowly add a cup of water.
  4. Place the dough on a stable surface and knead for 8-10 minutes.
  5. Take the kneaded dough and place it in a bowl, and then cover it with a cloth. Let the dough rise for an hour.
  6. While you're waiting for the dough, preheat your oven to 250Â°C.
  7. When the wait time for the dough is over, take it and make a round disc shape.
  8. Add the toppings: spread the tomato sauce all over the dough, but make sure to leave some space around the edge for the crust.
  9. Next, place the mozzarella on top of the tomato sauce, and add the desired amount of oil on the toppings.
  10. Bake the pizza in the oven, until the crust becomes golden and the cheese is melted.
  11. After baking the pizza, add some basil on the top. Now, your pizza is ready!
