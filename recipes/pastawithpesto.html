Pasta with pesto

Description

By following this simple recipe, you will be able to make a dish of pasta with pesto.

Ingredients

  • A pack of pasta (trenette or linguine is better)
  • 50/100g of fresh basil
  • A pinch of coarse salt
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • 20g of pine nuts
  • Some extravirgin olive oil
  • 40g of grated Parmiggiano Reggiano cheese
  • 40g of grated pecorino cheese

Steps

  1. First, let's make the pesto sauce. Wash and dry the basil.
  2. Peel and chop the garlic. After that, place the garlic inside a traditional mortar and grind. Then, place the basil leaves with a pinch of coarse salt and continue grinding.
  3. Crush the basil against the walls of the mortar by rolling the pestle in a rotary motion and rotating the mortar in the opposite direction.
  4. After the basil starts to form a green liquid like a uniform cream, add the pine nuts and the grated cheese and continue moving the pestle in order to make a creamier sauce.
  5. Finally, gradually add the extra virgin olive oil, repeating the same movement until you reach the desired consistency. Usually pesto should appear as a thick, light green cream.
  6. Start preparing the pasta according to the package instruction until al dente. When the pasta is ready, you can keep a little amount of pasta cooking water to pour in the pesto in order to change its consistency (it should not be too liquid!).
  7. Mix the pasta with the pesto sauce in pan (it's best to use the same pan that you used to cook the pasta).
  8. Now, you can serve your pasta with pesto, according to the desidered portions. Here's a tip: you can add a leaf of basil on top to have a delicious look!
